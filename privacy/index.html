Privacy Policy

Uchi is made by Mimis Code. This policy says what we collect, why, and what we never do.

The short version

  • Your chores, cards, and history sync to our servers so your household shares one board.
  • You can use Uchi without an email. If you add one, we use it only to sign you in.
  • Selfies are used once to draw your character, then discarded. The photo itself is never stored on our servers.
  • We do not sell data, run ads, or use third-party trackers.

What we do not do

  • No ads, analytics SDKs, data brokers, or selling/sharing data for marketing.
  • No reading your contacts, location, or photo library beyond the single image you pick.
  • Household data is only visible to members of your household, enforced with database row-level security.

Third-party processors

  • Supabase: database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Google Gemini API: receives the selfie if you use that feature, and card scene images solely to generate artwork.

Your controls

  • Save or stay anonymous: email is optional; anonymous accounts work fully.
  • Delete your account: Method tab → Account → Delete account.
  • Invites: invite codes expire and are single-use.

Children

Uchi is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.
